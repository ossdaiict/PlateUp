Privacy Policy

PlateUp • Smart Campus Cafeteria Ordering

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

PlateUp ("the App") is a smart campus cafeteria ordering platform developed as an open-source initiative under OSS DA-IICT for DAU (formerly DA-IICT).

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you regarding your information.

Your privacy matters to us. PlateUp only collects information necessary to provide campus cafeteria services and does not sell your personal information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in using Google, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Google account UID
  • Phone number provided during registration

Order Information

  • Ordered menu items
  • Canteen details
  • Order timestamps
  • Order status
  • Payment status
  • Pickup verification status

Payment Information

Payments are securely processed through supported payment providers including Razorpay and Paytm.

PlateUp never stores debit card numbers, credit card details, UPI PINs, banking passwords, or similar sensitive payment credentials.

Camera Access

Camera permission is requested solely for scanning QR codes during order pickup verification.

No photos or videos are stored or uploaded by PlateUp.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate users securely
  • Process cafeteria orders
  • Manage student, vendor and administrator accounts
  • Display order history
  • Enable QR pickup verification
  • Provide real-time order updates
  • Maintain application security and reliability

Data Storage

PlateUp uses trusted Google Firebase services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Firebase Cloud Functions

All communication between the application and backend services is encrypted using HTTPS.

Third-Party Services

PlateUp integrates with trusted third-party services necessary for application functionality.

  • Google Authentication
  • Firebase
  • Razorpay
  • Paytm

These services operate according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including Firebase Authentication, Firebase Security Rules, encrypted network communication, and role-based access controls.

While every effort is made to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Data Retention

Account and order information may be retained while your account remains active or as required for operational, administrative, or legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

PlateUp is designed for members of the university community and is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the project maintainers through the official GitHub repository:

https://github.com/ossdaiict/PlateUp
