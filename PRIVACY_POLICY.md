# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 16, 2026

## Introduction

PlateUp ("the App") is a smart campus cafeteria ordering platform developed as an open-source initiative under **OSS DA-IICT** for DAU (formerly DA-IICT).

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you regarding your information.

By using PlateUp, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

# Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the application, PlateUp may collect the following information:

## Account Information

When you sign in using your Google account, we may collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Google account identifier (UID)

During registration, we also collect:

- Phone number

This information is used solely to create and manage your PlateUp account.

---

## Order Information

When placing food orders, we store information including:

- Ordered items
- Selected canteen
- Order timestamps
- Order status
- Payment status
- Pickup verification status

This information is required to process and manage cafeteria orders.

---

## Payment Information

PlateUp integrates with third-party payment providers such as:

- Razorpay
- Paytm

PlateUp **does not store** your debit card, credit card, UPI PIN, banking credentials, or other sensitive payment information.

Payment processing is handled entirely by the respective payment provider according to their own privacy policies.

---

## Camera Access

PlateUp requests access to your device camera solely for QR code scanning during order pickup verification.

Images or video captured by the camera are **not stored**, uploaded, or shared.

---

# How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for purposes necessary to operate PlateUp, including:

- Authenticating users
- Managing student, vendor, and administrator accounts
- Processing cafeteria orders
- Displaying order history
- Managing canteens and menus
- Processing order pickups
- Sending operational notifications
- Maintaining application security and reliability

We do not sell your personal information.

---

# Data Storage

PlateUp stores application data using Google Firebase services, including:

- Firebase Authentication
- Firebase Realtime Database
- Firebase Cloud Functions

All communication between the application and Firebase services is encrypted using HTTPS.

---

# Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information is shared only when necessary to provide application functionality, including:

- Google (Authentication)
- Firebase (Backend Services)
- Razorpay (Payments)
- Paytm (Payments)

These providers process information according to their own privacy policies.

---

# Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

- Firebase Authentication
- Firebase Security Rules
- Encrypted network communication (HTTPS)
- Role-based access controls for students, vendors, and administrators

While we strive to protect your information, no electronic storage or transmission method is completely secure.

---

# Data Retention

Your account and order information may be retained while your account remains active or as required for operational, administrative, or legal purposes.

You may request deletion of your account by contacting the project maintainers.

---

# Children's Privacy

PlateUp is intended for members of the university community and is not designed for children under the age of 13.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
